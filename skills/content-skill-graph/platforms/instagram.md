# Instagram — Visual-First, 7-Slide Carousels

## Role
The visual lesson. Swipeable, saveable, shareable.

## Audience
- Visual learners
- Builders who screenshot tips
- Younger CTOs/founders

## DNA
- **Angle:** Visual gap or framework
- **Hook:** Slide 1 = bold claim or question
- **Tone:** Casual authority, teaching mode
- **Format:** 7-slide carousel (PDF or images)
- **Depth:** One framework, visually broken down

## Rules

### Must Have
- Hook on slide 1 (large text, high contrast)
- Progress indicator (1/7, 2/7, etc.)
- Actionable slide before close
- Summary/checklist on final slide

### Never
- Walls of text per slide
- More than 3 ideas per slide
- Low contrast text
- Generic stock photos

## Slide Structure

```
Slide 1: Hook (bold claim/question)
Slide 2: Context (the problem)
Slide 3: Gap (what's missing)
Slide 4: Framework (step 1)
Slide 5: Framework (step 2)
Slide 6: Framework (step 3)
Slide 7: Summary + CTA ("Save this", "Follow for more")
```

## Hook Formulas

### 1. The Question
> "Why does your [thing] keep failing?"

### 2. The Checklist
> "3 signs your [thing] is broken"

### 3. The Comparison
> "Your [A] vs. Your [B] — the gap is the problem"

### 4. The Number
> "[X]% of teams skip this. Don't."

## Visual Specs

- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:5 (1080x1350) or 1:1 (1080x1080)
- **Fonts:** Bold, sans-serif, high contrast
- **Colors:** Dark background, light text (or brand colors)
- **Text per slide:** 10-25 words max

## Caption Formula

```
Hook (first line, no "..." truncation)
↓
Brief context (2-3 sentences)
↓
Key takeaway
↓
CTA (Save, Share, Comment)
↓
3-5 relevant hashtags
```

## Examples

### Bad (Reformat)
> "New blog post about AI governance! Link in bio. #AI #Tech"

### Good (Rethink)
**Slide 1:** "Your Code vs. Your Prompts"
**Slide 2:** "Code: Versioned ✅ Reviewed ✅ Tested ✅"
**Slide 3:** "Prompts: Slack threads ❌ Notion docs ❌ Hope ❌"
**Slide 4:** "The EU AI Act doesn't care about your intentions"
**Slide 5:** "It wants: 1. Versioning 2. Approval gates 3. Traceability"
**Slide 6:** "Map → Version → Trace (same rigor as code)"
**Slide 7:** "Save this. Fix the gap before auditors ask."

**Caption:** "Most teams version their code but treat prompts like chat messages. That gap is where compliance goes to die. Swipe for the 3-step fix. Save this for your next audit prep. #AIGovernance #Engineering #Compliance #PromptEngineering"

## Repurposing Notes

| From Blog | Instagram Transformation |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| Long-form argument | Visual comparison |
| Text explanation | Framework diagram |
| Nuanced discussion | 3-step checklist |
| Abstract concepts | Concrete before/after |

## Validation Checklist
- [ ] Hook on slide 1 (readable in 2 seconds)?
- [ ] 7 slides (or justified fewer)?
- [ ] Progress indicators on each slide?
- [ ] Under 25 words per slide?
- [ ] Final slide has summary + CTA?
- [ ] Caption has hook + context + CTA?
- [ ] Visual contrast high enough for mobile?

## Platform-Specific Voice
Same tired engineer. But now they're at a whiteboard, marker in hand, drawing the thing you need to understand. Less talking, more showing.
